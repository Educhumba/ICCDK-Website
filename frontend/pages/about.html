About Us

Our vision, mission, and mandate.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for ethical commerce and sustainable development in Kenya and across the OIC community.

Our Mission

To strengthen economic ties, promote ethical business practices, and empower our members through advocacy, capacity building, and partnerships.

Our Mandate

Representing the interests of our members, business, and stakeholders.

Organizing trade missions, exhibitions, and B2B meetings to expand market access.

Providing training, workshops, and resources to help businesses thrive.

Facilitating the process for businesses to obtain recognized halal certification for their products and services.

Offering mediation and arbitration services for commercial disputes.

Highlighting investment opportunities and connecting local businesses with international investors.
