Our Services

We help your business grow — ethically and sustainably.

We guide businesses through the process of obtaining internationally recognized halal certification, opening up new markets and building consumer trust.

We help members navigate international trade regulations, find new business partners, and participate in global supply chains.

Our experts provide consultation on a range of business topics, from market entry strategies to corporate governance.

We showcase investment opportunities in Kenya and help link local projects with potential investors from OIC countries.

We host workshops and training programs on topics like ethical finance, Islamic marketing, and e-commerce.
